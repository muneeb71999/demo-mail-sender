WHO ARE WE

FOR VISION WITH TRUST

Visus Trust Limited ("Visus") is a specialized trust company incorporated in Hong Kong pursuant to and governed by the Companies Ordinance and Trustee Ordinance.

The founders (namely the ultimate beneficial shareholders) and executives of Visus are a group of well established lawyers (practising the law of Hong Kong, the mainland of China, England and the US), accountants, tax advisors, operators of family offices and directors of listed companies. More information about them is set out in the section headed “Our People”.

Visus provides a wide range of trust related services to our clients, friends, their families and charities ("Service Users ").
